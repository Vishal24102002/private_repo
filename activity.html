Yoga

Experience the serenity of early morning or sunset yoga sessions led by experienced instructors. Surrounded by the lush greenery of our farm, these sessions blend asanas, pranayama, and mindfulness to refresh your spirit and improve your physical well-being.

Meditation Relaxation

Step away from the chaos of daily life. Our guided meditation sessions focus on mindfulness, gratitude, and inner peace, making it an ideal way to reset and recharge. For ultimate relaxation, enjoy sessions under the open sky or in our dedicated meditation spaces.

Breathwork

Discover the transformative power of your breath with our guided sessions. Learn techniques to release stress, boost energy, and enhance emotional clarity while connecting deeply with yourself.

Dance Movement

Shake off inhibitions and move to the rhythm of your soul. Our expressive dance activities are not just a workout but a joyful celebration of life. Perfect for individuals or groups, these sessions leave you energized and uplifted.

Art activity

Unleash your creativity through painting, sketching, or crafts using natural and recycled materials. These activities are designed to inspire and reconnect you with the creative joy we often lose in our busy lives.
