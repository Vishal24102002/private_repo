WORKSHOPS

  1. Home
  2. Workshop

Our workshops offer an in-depth exploration of various interests and skills, all in the tranquil setting of our eco farm. Held on weekends, they provide a perfect way to spend a weekend immersed in learning and personal growth.

  • Spiritual and Personal Development: These sessions blend modern psychological techniques with ancient wisdom. Topics include mindfulness practices, goal setting, and self-discovery. Ideal for those seeking balance, clarity, and deeper meaning in their lives.
  • Dance & Music: Learn the art of movement and sound. Dance workshops are free movement, while music sessions include drumming, instrument basics, and vocal techniques.
  • Singing: Whether you're a seasoned singer or a complete beginner, our workshops focus on unlocking your voice. Group harmonies and solo singing opportunities ensure a supportive and fun experience.
  • Creative Activities: Explore the joy of crafting with your hands. Learn how to create beautiful items from clay, upcycled materials, or natural resources. From handmade jewelry to painting masterpieces, these sessions are perfect for letting your imagination run wild.
