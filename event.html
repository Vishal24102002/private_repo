EVENTS

  2. Events
We organize monthly events in collaboration with special guests, offering a blend of cultural, artistic, and wellness experiences.
  • Artist Events: Get inspired by talented musicians, dancers, and performers from around the globe. These events combine entertainment with artistry, creating unforgettable evenings under the stars.
  • Wellness Events: Take a step towards holistic health with workshops and interactive sessions led by wellness experts. From sound healing and aromatherapy to herbal remedies, these events are designed to rejuvenate and restore balance.
  • Eco Farm Exploration: Engage with nature like never before! Learn about sustainable farming techniques, participate in farm-to-table activities, and discover how you can live more harmoniously with the earth.
  • Festivals: Celebrate Goa’s vibrant culture with festivals filled with music, dance, and community spirit. From harvest celebrations to eco-conscious holiday events, each festival is a unique blend of tradition and joy.
  • Market Days: Stroll through a vibrant marketplace featuring local artisans, organic produce, handmade crafts, and eco-friendly products. Support the local community while indulging in unique finds and delicious treats.
