Support HEM Eco Farmstay: Be Part of Our Sustainable Vision

At HEM Eco Farmstay, we’re more than just a place to unwind; we’re a community dedicated to living in harmony with nature. Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism and conserve biodiversity while offering a tranquil getaway for nature lovers.

Why We Need Your Support

To expand and sustain our initiatives, we need your help. Your donation will directly contribute to: Eco-Friendly Infrastructure: Building sustainable facilities powered by renewable energy. Conservation Efforts: Protecting native flora and fauna and nurturing biodiversity. Community Development: Supporting local artisans, farmers, and educational workshops. Visitor Experience: Enhancing eco-tourism activities like organic farming, forest walks, and permaculture learning.

Every Contribution Counts

Whether it’s ₹100 or ₹10,000, your contribution will create a ripple effect of positive change. With your help, we can build a greener, more sustainable future—one step at a time.

Make a Donation


You can also support us by spreading the word about HEM Eco Farmstay to your friends and family.

Thank You for Making a Difference! Your generosity fuels our passion for sustainability and community growth. Together, we can create a lasting impact.
